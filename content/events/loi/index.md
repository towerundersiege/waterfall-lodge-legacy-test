# Lodge of Instruction

Waterfall Lodge of Instruction is open to members of the lodge and invited Master Masons.

Current and historic Lodge of Instruction dates are now maintained on the main [Events](/events/) page so the schedule, locations and notes stay in one place.

If you would like to attend a Lodge of Instruction meeting, please contact the Lodge Secretary in advance.
