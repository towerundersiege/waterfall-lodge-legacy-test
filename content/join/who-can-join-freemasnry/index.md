<p style="text-align: center;"><b>Who can join the Freemasons?</b></p>
<p>Membership is open to men over the age of 21 years, of all faiths who are law-abiding, of good character and who acknowledge a belief in God.</p>
<p>Freemasonry is a multi-racial and multi-cultural organization. It has attracted men of goodwill from all sectors of the community into membership.</p>
