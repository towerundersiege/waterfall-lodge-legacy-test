title: About

Waterfall Lodge is a small, traditional lodge with an emphasis on fellowship, charity, and personal development.

We welcome men who are curious about Freemasonry and want to understand whether the Craft is a good fit for them. The lodge values clear communication, steady progress, and a calm atmosphere where members can take part at a pace that suits them.

If you would like to learn more, the Freemasonry and Join pages explain the practical side of membership and what to expect.
